Student summer Internship

The Work


This summer we are looking for a university student who is interested in full stack web development and distributed energy systems. The focus of this internship for the 2018-19 summer period will be software development within a modern web based front end for a Solar PV Asset Management system. The software is used to efficiently manage many large distributed Solar PV systems.

JavaScript skills will be essential and knowledge of NodeJS and AWS tools such as Lambda and DynamoDB would also be useful.

The software you will be working on during the internship is built on top of the Open Source SolarNetwork platform and the team uses the Eclipse development environment.

Greenstage is a small distributed company with NZ based team members in Christchurch, Wellington and Auckland. This internship is Auckland based and you will be working with our small team in St Johns.

Apply


If this opportunity interests you, please send your CV and a cover letter by 30th November to: internship@greenstage.co.nz
