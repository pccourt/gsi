Supporters and sponsors

Supporters, how can you help us?

There are two easy ways you can support us:

1) Purchase and wear our stylish merchandise. From as little as $19 you can show your support. Go to the Merchandise section to make your choices.

2) Become a registered supporter. For $150 per year registered supporters are provided with an official signed and dated supporters poster and may choose to be listed on our greenstage supporters web page (web page listing is optional). Additionally all supporters go into a draw to win an invite to one of our track testing sessions and to experience the amazing GS750V first hand as our driver puts the car through it's paces.

Sponsors Marketing Opportunities

Here at greenstage we look for synergy in all our relationships and this is just as true with the marketing and sponsorship opportunities we have on offer.

Our initial goal is to get to the race track and prove the capability of the GS750V. We have recenlty refocused on competing in hill climb events before steping up to the longer races in the NZSC serires (stay tuned in 2011). It is also possible that we will become involved in other activities as time permits (e.g. drag racing and drifting).

The performance, energy source and energy efficiency aspects of this car are very enticing to a wider variety of audiences than your typical race car. As a result the car and the racing campaign are likely to generate a large amount of public interest both in NZ and abroad.

We have big plans and if you want to be involved in the success of these plans, either actively or passively, or you want to leverage the resulting marketing opportunities, please let us know. Contact Philip Court on +64 21 953 032 (NZST) or email philip@greenstage.co.nz to discuss our opportunities and your ideas and see what synergies we can find.
